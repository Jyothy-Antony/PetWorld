LICENSE PROCEDURE FOR DOGS IN KERALA

People who keep pet dogs at their home are required to apply for license within one month.

Application should be submitted to Panchayath / Municipality/ Corporation Secretary.

Veterinary Surgeon's certificate about the vaccination given should be filed with the application.

The license fee is Rs 10 for Panchayath and Rs 50 for Municipality / Corporation.
